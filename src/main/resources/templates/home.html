Why Using MUCPMS

The capstone project is one of the most important stages in a student's life, where they showcase their skills and the culmination of what they've learned. Given its significance, it needs to be organized on a single platform to simplify the process for students. Instead of burdening them with the need to submit each stage through different applications, this website centralizes everything, making the capstone project stage more streamlined and efficient.

Capstone Project Journey

Stage 1: Selecting Your Partner

Your capstone partner is more than just a collaborator—they’re your partner in success.
Choose wisely! Select someone you have strong chemistry with, who shares your passion,
and who will drive you both toward achieving great results together.

Stage 2: Selecting Project Idea

Choosing the right capstone project idea is a critical step in your academic journey. It should be a topic that excites you and aligns with your career goals. Focus on real-world problems that spark your curiosity, while also considering the feasibility and resources available. A well-chosen project idea will not only challenge you but also showcase your skills and innovation, setting you apart in your field.

Stage 5: Survey Similar Applications

Survey similar applications and learn from them to help you shape your detailed functional requirements. Analyze their strengths and weaknesses, and identify key features that could enhance your own project. This research will provide valuable insights to refine your design and user experience.
